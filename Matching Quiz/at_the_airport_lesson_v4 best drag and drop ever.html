📖 Reading Passage

🖱️ Hover over any highlighted word to see its Arabic meaning.

Ali goes to the airport. He has a passport and a ticket. He goes to the check-in desk. He says, "Hello." The woman says, "Passport, please." Ali gives his passport and ticket. She gives him a boarding pass. Ali says, "Thank you."

Now Ali goes to the gate. He waits there. Then he gets on the plane. He finds his seat. He sits down. He is happy. He is ready for his flight.
